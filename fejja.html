Fejja - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

This privacy policy explains how Fejja ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information when you use our photo editing and filtering mobile application.

Permissions and Data Usage

Fejja requests the following permissions to provide its core functionality:

Camera Access: Required to capture new photos directly within the app for editing and applying filters.
Photo Library Access: Required to select existing photos from your device for editing and to save processed images back to your photo library.
Storage Access: Required to temporarily store and process images during the editing workflow.
Location Access (Optional): Used for location-based filters and effects. This permission is optional and can be denied without affecting core app functionality.
In-App Purchases: Required to process credit purchases and premium subscriptions through your device's app store.

Data Collection and Processing

Local Processing

Your photos and images are processed locally on your device whenever possible. We prioritize your privacy by minimizing data transmission.

Cloud Processing

For advanced AI-powered filters and effects, images may be securely transmitted to our processing servers. In these cases:

  • Images are encrypted during transmission
  • Processed images are automatically deleted from our servers after processing
  • No images are stored permanently on our servers
  • Images are not used for training AI models or any other purposes

Account Information

If you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for account management and communication)
  • Account preferences and settings
  • Purchase history for subscription and credit management
  • Usage statistics to improve app performance

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information or images with third parties, except:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party services that help us operate the app (e.g., cloud processing, payment processing)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and users' safety
  • App Store Partners: Purchase information shared with Apple App Store or Google Play Store for transaction processing

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal information by our team

Data Retention

  • Images: Deleted immediately after processing (cloud) or stored locally on your device only
  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period after deletion
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained for app improvement purposes

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection
  • Withdraw consent for optional permissions

Children's Privacy

Fejja is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new privacy policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Third-Party Services

Fejja integrates with the following third-party services:

  • App Stores: Apple App Store and Google Play Store for app distribution and in-app purchases
  • AI Processing: Third-party AI services for advanced image processing (images are not stored by these services)
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage analytics to improve app performance
